  • How to generate and ship a good idea

    Generating ideas is not as simple as it might seem, in fact I've had hundreds of ideas but only have built maybe 5 or 6 of them, why?

    Well, you only have such little time and knowledge. Some ideas that I've had and successfully launched were Colortopia, Hidden Tools, and many more.

    The process of having an idea is hard. Look at the image below, you should be having questions while you have the idea, before you start building it, marketing it, etc.


    "Okay, I get it. How can I build my idea though?"

    Now that you have a solid idea, you can move on. Building it, you can simply do this by either coding the project or using a nocode tool such as Webflow, to build it.

    That part is pretty self-explanatory, you just make the project, do the UI, etc.
